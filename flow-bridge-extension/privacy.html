Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 13, 2026

1. Introduction

PromptAgent Flow Bridge ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension handles data and ensures your information remains secure.

2. Data Collection & Usage

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to any external servers.
  • Local Data Only: Any data processed by the extension (such as credit estimation settings or prompt configurations) is stored locally on your device using browser storage.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Since we do not collect your data, we do not sell, trade, or share any information with third parties.

3. Permissions Disclosure

To provide its core functionality, the extension requires permissions for storage (to save your settings), tabs/windows (to manage the split-view workspace), and host permissions (to interact with Gemini and Google Flow platforms).

4. Data Security

All data stays within your browser's secure environment. We do not use any external analytics or tracking scripts that could monitor your browsing activity.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our official support channels.
